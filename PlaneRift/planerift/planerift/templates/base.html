Planechase Ruling

How to play

On your turn, roll the die.
One may role again
Cost is one extra mana per roll:

cost = rolls - 1


Why roll the die?

Rolling the die could have three results:
Blank: nothing happens.
Chaos: chaos ability triggers.
Planechase: planes change.
